Nomster

Manage and synchronize your daily lunch with your peers

What's Nomster?

The reason why Nomster exists: We needed an usecase to experiment with something new. Nomster is an approach to simplify the "what shall we eat at lunch time?" process. It provides a CRUD-mechanism for lunch ideas. The project has a lossly tied module system.
